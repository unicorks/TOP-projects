Facts about flowers!

A field of sunflowers! Each sunflower is not one, but many flowers combined.

A pretty pink carnation! Carnations come in almost every colour.

A bushy dahlia! Dahlias rely on their bright petals to attract insects.

A blue plumbago! Butterflies love to feed off its nectar.
